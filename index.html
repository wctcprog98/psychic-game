RULES OF THE GAME

A random letter known as the secret letter will be selected once the game starts. The letter will be hidden from you and you will be allowed 10 chances to guess the correct letter. You can guess letters simply by typing your guess on the keyboard. So for example if you want to guess the letter 'a', just type 'a' on the keyboard. Guess the correct letter before you run out of guesses to win the game! Press START to begin.
